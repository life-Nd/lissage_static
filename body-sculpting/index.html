Cryolipolysis or Cryotherapy -

Cryolipolysis (”Coldsculpting”) is a technique that uses cold to freeze fat and sculpt the body. This non-invasive technique is gentle and safe. Cryolipolysis eliminates stubborn fat quickly and without surgery. Compared to traditional liposuction, cryolipolysis is safer, much less painful and requires less recovery time.
Various parts of the body can be treated, such as the stomach, chin, flanks, upper back, thighs, buttocks, pectorals, or arms. One or two sessions per area are usually sufficient, but depending on the desired objective, it is possible to take the experience a step further and sculpt the body using the various applicators available
Contraindications

Pregnant or breast-feeding women should postpone this type of treatment, as should anyone suffering from an abdominal hernia.

Muscle Toning (EMSculpt)

EMSCULPT technology is based on applicators that simultaneously emit synchronized RF and HIFEM+ energies. This latest-generation procedure simultaneously delivers radiofrequency heat and magnetic energy, resulting in greater fat reduction and a significant increase in muscle mass (according to clinical studies, on average a 25% increase in muscle volume and a 30% reduction in fat*)
Can you do 20,000 crunches in 30 minutes? That’s what Em-sculpt RF does for you! The electromagnetic energy used forces muscles to contract far more intensely than you could do on your own during a regular exercise session. After 30 minutes, you’ll have done the equivalent of 20,000 sit-ups or squats, with no effort at all.
Optimal results are generally achieved after 4 to 5 sessions of around 30 minutes each, spaced 2 to 3 days apart. Please note that results may vary from one person to another.

Contraindications

This treatment is not recommended for pregnant women, people with pacemakers or metal implants such as copper IUDs
